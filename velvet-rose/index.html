Velvet Rose beauty collective

A small, hand-picked group of independent artists working under one quiet, considered roof — brows, lashes, permanent makeup, and all the rituals in between.

Meet the artists About the studio

Built for artists who care about their craft.

Velvet Rose Beauty Collective was founded by Kenzie — a permanent makeup artist who wanted a beauty space that felt as considered as the work happening inside it.

Every artist here runs their own books, sets their own hours, and brings their own clientele. What we share is the room: soft light, warm details, florals on every surface, and a quiet that’s rare in a beauty studio.

If you’re a guest, you’ll find a calm place to land before your appointment. If you’re an artist looking for a chair, you’ll find a community.

— Kenzie
