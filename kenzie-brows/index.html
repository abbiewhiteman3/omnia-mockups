What to expect

A calm, considered
experience.

i.

Consultation

We’ll talk through your goals, your skin, and what’s realistic for your face. No deposits required.

ii.

Mapping & design

Brows or lips are drawn on first — we don’t start until you love what you see in the mirror.

iii.

The session

Roughly three hours, with breaks. Numbing throughout. Most clients describe it as relaxing.

iv.

Healing & touch-up

Color settles over 4–6 weeks. A touch-up perfects shape and saturation.
