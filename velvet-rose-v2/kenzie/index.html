Founder · Permanent Makeup & Esthetics

Kenzie

Kenzie is the founder of Velvet Rose Beauty Collective and the artist behind Kenzie Brows — a permanent makeup artist, esthetician, and cosmetic tattoo artist based in Salt Lake City.

Her work is soft, considered, and always tailored to your face — never templated. Every appointment starts with a real conversation: face mapping, color matching, and time to talk through what you actually want. New-client consultations are always complimentary.
