Our Story

Built for artists who care about their craft.

Velvet Rose was founded by Kenzie — a permanent makeup artist, esthetician, and cosmetic tattoo artist who wanted a beauty space that felt as considered as the work happening inside it.

Every artist here runs their own books, sets their own hours, and brings their own clientele. What we share is the room: soft light, warm details, and a quiet that's rare in a beauty studio.

If you're a guest, you'll find a calm place to land. If you're looking for a chair, you'll find a community.

Soft. Considered. Always yours.

— Kenzie
