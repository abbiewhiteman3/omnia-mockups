The Story

Beauty with backbone.

B0ld Beauty started with a simple idea: a place in Lawrenceville where women could come exactly as they are and leave feeling exactly as they want to. A fuchsia suite, no waiting room small talk, no rushed appointments — just one client at a time, treated like the only client of the day.

Every service is mine from start to finish. Brows, lashes, wax, skin — all under one roof, all built around the kind of details that don't get rushed and don't get skipped. You're not booking a service. You're booking time that's all yours.
